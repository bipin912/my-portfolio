Online Esports Registration System(OERS)

Online Esports Registration System (OERS) is the advanced online registration system used for esports tournament registration working on the mobile platform. It is developed under flutter using Dart Programming language along with Firebase as cloud platform. For the well managed tournaments, proper registration and team evaluation is needed. OERS helps to register authenticate players to participate in the esport tournaments through face authentication. OERS helps in evaluation of random players seeking for participation according to their ranks for balanced matchmaking.
